Z Fellows 2026 Proposal
ROS2 Copilot
Cursor, but for robotics engineers.
ROS2 is the Robot Operating System powering Boston Dynamics, autonomous vehicles, and industrial automation — and it's notoriously painful to work with. Engineers lose days debugging cryptic error logs, fighting dependency conflicts, and hunting through fragmented forums for one-line answers buried in 2021 threads.

ROS2 Copilot reads your error logs, understands your ROS2 distribution and workspace context, and tells you exactly what broke and how to fix it in under 10 seconds. It also generates complete, idiomatic node boilerplate from plain English — so engineers spend their time building robots, not fighting tooling.

I have been that engineer. This tool is built from real pain, not a gap analysis.
